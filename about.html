About Cliff

Huntsville Social Security Disability Attorney

My promise to you is to get to the facts and find the proof to help prove your disability claim to the Social Security Administration.

For more than a decade, I was an investigative professional. When it comes to finding the evidence necessary, my depth of experience is unique among attorneys. My promise to you is to get the facts and find the proof to help prove your disability claim to the Social Security Administration.

I grew up in a military family and served in the military myself before moving to Huntsville to work as an anchor and investigative reporter. While working at WAAY, I attended law school. Once I finished law school, I left my TV career behind and handled capital murder cases, federal drugs cases and other jury matters before I decided to concentrate primarily on Social Security Disability.

I am proud to say I’ve helped more than 1,500 people get the disability benefits they needed. If you cannot work due to a physical or mental condition, let me help you with your Social Security Disability case.
